# Lists

## Ordered list

```markdown
1.  topic 1
    1.  sub topic 1
    2.  sub topic 2
        1.  sub sub topic 1
        2.  sub sub topic 2
        3.  sub sub topic 3
```

<div class="result" markdown>

1.  topic 1
    1.  sub topic 1
    2.  sub topic 2
        1.  sub sub topic 1
        2.  sub sub topic 2
        3.  sub sub topic 3

</div>

## Unordered list

```markdown
- topic 1

  - sub topic 1
  - sub topic 2

    - sub sub topic 1
    - sub sub topic 2
    - sub sub topic 3
```

<div class="result" markdown>

- topic 1

  - sub topic 1
  - sub topic 2

    - sub sub topic 1
    - sub sub topic 2
    - sub sub topic 3

</div>
